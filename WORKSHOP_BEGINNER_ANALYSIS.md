## Summary: What's Missing Before Section 1

### NEW SECTION NEEDED: "SECTION 0: GET YOUR ACCOUNTS READY" (8:50–9:00am)

#### Step 1: Create a ChatGPT Account
- **What is it?** ChatGPT is an AI assistant that helps you write ideas, business names, and marketing copy.
- **Cost:** $20/month (or free with ChatGPT 3.5, but we recommend paid for this workshop)
- **Why we need it:** We'll use it to brainstorm your business idea, create your branding, and write your website copy.
- **Sign up here:** [Link to OpenAI.com/ChatGPT]
- **Instructions:**
  1. Click the link above
  2. Sign up with your email or Google account
  3. Verify your email
  4. Set up payment
  5. You're ready!

#### Step 2: Create a Vercel Account
- **What is it?** Vercel is a platform that hosts websites on the internet so people can visit them.
- **Cost:** Free for personal projects
- **Why we need it:** This is where we'll deploy (upload) your website to make it live.
- **Sign up here:** [Link to Vercel.com]
- **Instructions:**
  1. Click the link above
  2. Sign up with your GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket account (don't have one? See Step 3)
  3. Verify your account
  4. You're ready!

#### Step 3: Create a GitHub Account (Required for Vercel)
- **What is it?** GitHub is where developers store and manage code. Think of it like Google Drive, but for code.
- **Cost:** Free
- **Why we need it:** Vercel needs to connect to GitHub to store and deploy your website code.
- **Sign up here:** [Link to GitHub.com]
- **Instructions:**
  1. Click the link above
  2. Sign up with your email
  3. Verify your email
  4. Done!

#### Step 4: Quick Test - Make Sure Everything Works
- Try logging into each account to verify they work
- If you have any errors, ask now before the workshop starts
